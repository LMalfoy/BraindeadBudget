# Statistics Roadmap

## Current State

The Statistics area is now built around three perspectives:

- `Total Spending`
- `Budget Spending`
- `Recurring Spending`

It already includes:

- combined monthly overview
- spending by category
- budget trajectory
- month comparison
- carryover
- spending pattern
- fixed-cost distribution and ratio
- subscription load
- savings stability
- savings-based chess progression

## Product Role

Statistics should now do three things only:

- explain the current month clearly
- separate behavioral spending from recurring structure
- reward saved budget through progression without moralizing spending style

## Remaining Statistics Work

The remaining work in Stats is refinement, not expansion.

### 1. Budget Progression Polish

- keep the progression panel readable and secondary to the top overview
- continue improving explanation and presentation where needed

### 2. Small UX Refinements

- confirm module order still feels right in real use
- tighten empty states and sheet wording when needed
- ensure light and dark mode both present charts and piece assets clearly

### 3. Final QA

- verify the three-perspective layout remains understandable
- check that overview first, progression second, and detail views below still feels intentional
- keep charts readable without adding denser analytics

## Non-Goals

- no return to behavior-based scoring
- no hidden numeric score
- no large new analytics surface unless a real product gap appears
