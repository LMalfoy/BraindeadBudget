Objective
- Refine the Trends page layout for consistency, chart sizing, and clearer panel titles.

In Scope
- Make title positions stable across Trends panels
- Make chart positions stable across Trends panels
- Increase chart use of available panel space
- Use clearer titles for monthly spending and category trend panels
- Give category trend pages a fixed legend area sized for up to 9 items
- Keep carryover card aligned and visually consistent

Out of Scope
- New analytics
- New sections
- Changes to data calculations
- Changes outside the Trends page

Acceptance Criteria
- Trend panel titles sit at a stable vertical position
- Charts sit directly below titles at a stable vertical position
- Charts feel larger and better centered in their panels
- Monthly spending titles clearly communicate “monthly … over the last 6 months”
- Category trend pages have stable chart + legend layout even when legend length differs
